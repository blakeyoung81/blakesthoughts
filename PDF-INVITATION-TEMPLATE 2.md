## How to Create Your PDF

### Option 1: Use Canva (Easiest & Prettiest)
1. Go to https://www.canva.com/
2. Search "Invitation" templates
3. Choose a romantic design
4. Customize with your text
5. Download as PDF
6. Save to `/public/final-invitation.pdf`

**Recommended Canva templates:**
- "Dinner Invitation"
- "Date Night Card"
- "Romantic Invitation"
- "Elegant Event Invitation"

### Option 2: Google Docs/Pages
1. Create new document
2. Use large, romantic fonts
3. Add emojis and formatting
4. Center important information
5. Export/Download as PDF
6. Save to `/public/final-invitation.pdf`

**Font suggestions:**
- Headings: Cormorant Garamond, Playfair Display
- Body: Montserrat, Lato, Open Sans

### Option 3: Adobe Express (Free)
1. Go to https://www.adobe.com/express/
2. Choose "Invitation" template
3. Customize design
4. Download as PDF
5. Save to `/public/final-invitation.pdf`

### Option 4: Hand-Written (Most Romantic!)
1. Write invitation on nice paper
2. Make it beautiful and personal
3. Take a high-quality scan/photo
4. Convert to PDF
5. Save to `/public/final-invitation.pdf`
