# Ivy Med

**Ivy Med** is a premium medical education platform designed to assist medical students in mastering complex concepts and passing high-stakes board examinations like the USMLE Step 1.

## 🩺 Platform Focus

- **Board-Style Questions**: Realistic, high-yield practice questions designed to simulate official exam formats.
- **Explainers & Concept Mapping**: Dynamic visual and text-based guides linking medical topics (e.g. pathology, pharmacology, physiology).
- **AI-Powered Learning**: Integrates intelligent tutoring modules to clarify complex clinical scenarios in real time.

## 🛠️ Stack & Architecture

- **Astro**: High-performance, SEO-optimized framework driving the client-side experience.
- **React**: Dynamic component layers for interactive quizzes and dashboards.
- **Tailwind CSS**: Modern utility-first CSS styling.
