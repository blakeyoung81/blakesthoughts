# Restoring Hope Clinic

**Restoring Hope Clinic** is a community-oriented digital portal representing a volunteer-run healthcare clinic. It connects patients with scheduling services and secure intake options.

## 🏥 Clinical Digital Offerings

- **HIPAA-Friendly Forms**: Secure patient intake forms capturing essential health histories.
- **Provider Scheduling**: Internal administration portals showing volunteer doctor rotations and appointment slots.
- **Community Information**: Direct translation layers and accessible layouts highlighting free clinical services.

## 🛠️ Stack & Architecture

- **React**: Reusable layouts for pages and form states.
- **Node.js**: Backend validation and encrypted data relay channels.
- **Vanilla CSS**: Clean, accessible stylesheets supporting full screen-reader compliance.
