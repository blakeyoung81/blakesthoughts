# BoardBuddy

BoardBuddy is a Chrome extension built for medical students prepping with UWorld. It brings AI-powered analysis and on-page helpers to your practice questions so you can study faster, capture key takeaways, and keep your knowledge fresh.

## What it does

- **On-question insights:** Highlights question intent, key findings, and likely distractors while you work inside UWorld.
- **Smart recall prompts:** Generates rapid recall drills and mnemonics tailored to each vignette to reinforce memory.
- **Personalized study aids:** Suggests tutoring personas, study plans, and targeted follow-up questions based on your weak areas.
- **Session organization:** Keeps a running history of reviewed questions and exports summaries so you can revisit tricky topics.
- **Media support:** Handles rich content (images and diagrams) common in board-style questions while keeping the layout clean.
